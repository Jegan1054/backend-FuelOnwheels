Privacy Policy - Fuel On Wheels

Last updated: January 2026

Welcome to Fuel On Wheels. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information while using our mobile application.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • What information we collect
  • How your data is used
  • How we protect your data
  • Your rights as a user

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register or use the app, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Mobile number (used for login and verification)
  • Email address
  • User role (customer or service provider)

1.2 Location Information

  • User location to identify nearby fuel providers or service centers
  • Service provider location for availability and tracking purposes

1.3 App Usage Data

  • Device information
  • IP address
  • App interaction logs
  • Error reports and crash logs

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Authenticate users securely
  • Display nearby fuel and roadside assistance services
  • Enable communication between users and service providers
  • Provide chatbot-based answers to app-related questions
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Ensure safety and prevent misuse

3. Chatbot Usage

Fuel On Wheels includes a chatbot feature that is designed only to answer user questions related to:

  • App features
  • General service information
  • Common user queries

The chatbot does not place orders, process payments, or perform service bookings.

4. Data Security

We follow standard security practices such as:

  • Secure authentication systems
  • Encrypted network communication (HTTPS)
  • Restricted access to user data
  • Regular system monitoring

5. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (authentication and notifications)
  • Google Maps API (location services)

These services follow their own privacy policies and data protection standards.

6. User Rights

  • View and update your profile
  • Request correction of your data
